Spooky Investigation

Intro

Embark on a gripping sci-fi fantasy adventure as you take on the role of a brave and resourceful investigator on a quest to uncover the truth behind your brother's mysterious disappearance. Delve into a world of intrigue and danger as you explore dark and foreboding places, high-tech laboratories, and otherworldly landscapes, all while encountering a cast of strange and enigmatic characters.

Armed only with your wits and a trusty sword, you'll need to navigate treacherous encounters with all manner of bizarre and deadly creatures. Most encounters can be tackled in any order, providing a uniquely customizable experience that lets you tackle the challenges in the way that best suits your playstyle.

With gorgeous pixel graphics and an atmospheric original soundtrack, Spooky Investigation is a feast for the senses that will keep you on the edge of your seat from start to finish. Will you be able to solve the mystery and find your missing brother, or will you fall prey to the forces that seek to thwart your every move? The fate of the galaxy rests in your hands in Spooky Investigation.

Gameplay Features

  • Turn-based battles with a strategic combat system
  • An intrueing story with well-developed characters
  • A world with both fantasy and sci-fi elements
  • An equipment system that lets players find and equip new weapons, armor, and accessories to improve their characters' stats
  • New weapons unlcok new abilities in combat
  • An alignment system that lets players make choices that affect the story
  • High-quality pixel art and animation to bring the game world and characters to life.
  • Original soundtrack with memorable tunes that set the mood for different locations and events.

There's More

  • Multiple endings that depend on the choices you make in the game
  • Challenging boss battles that require careful strategy and preparation
  • Hidden secrets and easter eggs that reward exploration and experimentation
