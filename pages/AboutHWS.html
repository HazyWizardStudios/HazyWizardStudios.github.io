About Us

Hazy Wizard Studios Pillars

  1. Always give The Player what THEY want. Everything from gameplay, features and UI. have toggle options in a menu
  2. No Microtransactions, no DLC, only old-school style Expansions.
  3. Generally, design for PVP first.
  4. If there are choices, they MUST be meaningful
  5. RPG games will be based on the system we have (currently called Crown and Blood) and stay faithful to that.
  6. Stylized is usually more appropriate than realistic.
  7. Player shouldn't be confused about what to do or where to go. In-game journal if needed.
  8. Don't go crazy with the budget, keep prices reasonable. Aim for comparatively low prices, therefore offering lots of value.
  9. An extension of #1, appeal to casuals and ultra hardcore players. Simple but Deep.
  10. Devs should be enthusiastic. Avoid using purely mercenaries.
  11. Give players great rewards.
  12. Rule of Cool, almost always, but be consistent.
  13. Don't chase fads. Reference old games and non-video games.
  14. EXCELLENT music. go ALL OUT.
  15. Always make a demo. No stupid CGI trailers!
  16. Don't make things long just for the sake of it. No filler.
  17. Games should usually be replayable.
  18. Games should be complete and run well on release. Early Access if needed.
