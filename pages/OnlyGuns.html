Only Guns

Intro

Sick of boring games that feel like chores? This one is different.

All we care about is making a fun, compelling experience.

You think you're good at video games? Here's a chance to prove it.

Game Pillars

  • Pistol and Sniper only. Jump, ledge grab and mantle.
  • Give The Player an opportunity to be skillful and make it feel good.
  • Challenging but not frustrating. Varying levels of difficulty options.
  • Violence. Speed. Momentum.
  • It's competitive, highscore board.
  • Humor. Some things are just totally absurd. FUN! Rule of cool!
  • Keep things simple and small so the game runs well.
  • A little jank is fine, even adds character
  • "Vocal Barks" from Sgt Impolite, game is like a parody of military training.

Specifics

  • Four Unique and distinct Levels
  • Various puzzles, platforming areas, and sections that combine both.
  • On-screen timer. Checkpoints, but they change with difficulty. Easy has many. Ultra has none.
